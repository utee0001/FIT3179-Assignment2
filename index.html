World Happy Score, GDP, Life Expectancy Visualisation

World Happiness Score in the year of 2019

This interactive map provides details about the happiness score for countries around the world in the year of 2019. The slider filter can filtercountries with Happiness Score under a specific value

From adjusting the Happiness Score in the slider bar, it shows that countries with low happiness score are mainly found in Africa and some countries in South Asia. Low happiness score can also be impacted by other factor such as low GDP, which will be explained in the next section. On the other hand, countries that have high happiness score are mainly found in North America, Oceania and Europe. Meanwhile, Happiness score of most countries in Asia lies between 4 to 6.

The relation between GDP, Happiness Score and Life Expectancy

GDP and Life Expectancy socre are two of the many factors that are used in the caluclation of Happiness Score of each countries.This visualisation proves that countries with higher GDP have higher happiness score as well. However, there are outliers - Botswana and Costa Rica. As we can see the, the GDP of these 2 countries are very similar. However, the different in their happiness score is significant. Even GDP is one of the main factor that affects Happiness Score of a Country, but other factors can also have significant impact on happiness score of a country as well.

Life Expectancy score is also one of the factor that contribute to the calucaltion of happiness score. However it does not have a big impact as what GDP has on Happiness Score. As mentioned above, a country with higher GDP would normally has higher Happiness Score as well, but this relationship does not exist between happiness score and life expectancy. Through adjust the selection menu of Life Expectancy in the visualisation, we can see that in each level of life expectancy there are countries with significant different in happiness score and GDP
